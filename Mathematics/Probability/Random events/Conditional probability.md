# Conditional probability
$$P(X|Y)=\frac{P(X\cap Y)}{P(Y)}$$
## Properties
1. $P(X|Y)\geq 0$
2. $P(Y|Y)=1$
3. $P(A\cup B|Y)=P(A|Y)+P(B|Y)\text{ while A and B are mutually exclusive}$

## Independence
$$P(A\cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B)$$
$$P(A|B)=P(A)$$
