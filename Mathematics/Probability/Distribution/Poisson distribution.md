# Poisson distribution
$$X\sim Poisson(\lambda T)$$
- same as [[Binomial distribution]] but $n$ is extremely high and $p$ is extremely low
## Discrete form
[[Probability mass function|PMF]]: 
$$p_X(x)=e^{-\lambda T}\cdot\frac{(\lambda T)^x}{x!}$$

[[Gamma distribution]]
